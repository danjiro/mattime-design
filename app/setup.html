One way to calculate your existing hours: Average hours training GI a week * (number of weeks you've been training - number of weeks taken off for injuries/breaks).

Eg: If you've been training for 2.5 years with an average of 4 hours of GI mattime a week and about 3 weeks off a year: 4 * (52(weeks in a year) * 2.5 - (3 * 2.5)) = 490 GI hours
